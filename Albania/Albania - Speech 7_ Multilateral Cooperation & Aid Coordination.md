Honorable Chair, Distinguished Delegates,  
The delegation of Albania believes that protecting the victims of this conflict is a challenge too great for any single organization to solve alone.  
While NATO provides essential security and logistical support, we must recognize that other actors possess unique humanitarian expertise. To achieve the best results, we must avoid duplication of effort and build a coordinated response.  
Therefore, Albania proposes that NATO should establish a Joint Coordination Task Force. This platform would actively share logistical data and security assessments with the United Nations, the European Union, and NGOs like the Red Cross.  
For instance, NATO can secure transport routes, while the European Union provides financial aid, and the UN coordinates the distribution of food and medicine. This complementary approach ensures that our resources are used in the most efficient way possible.  
In conclusion, our strength lies not only in our military power but also in our ability to cooperate. Let us build bridges between international organizations to create a stronger safety net for civilians.  
Thank you, and I yield my time back to the Chair.
