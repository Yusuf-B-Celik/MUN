Honorable Chair, Distinguished Delegates,  
The delegation of Albania believes that protecting the victims of war is not only about providing immediate food and shelter. It is also about seeking justice and ensuring accountability for those who violate international law.  
During this conflict, we have seen unacceptable attacks on critical civilian infrastructure, including schools, hospitals, and energy networks. These are not military targets; these are war crimes under International Humanitarian Law.  
Albania strongly supports the efforts of international courts to investigate these actions. We believe that NATO can play a supporting role by securing and sharing high-quality monitoring data to help document these violations.  
Furthermore, Albania supports initiatives like the "Register of Damage" to ensure that victims eventually receive compensation for what they have lost. We must send a clear message to the world: those who target innocent civilians will be held responsible.  
We cannot have a lasting, just peace without justice for the victims.  
Thank you, and I yield my time back to the Chair.
